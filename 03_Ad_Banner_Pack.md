## BANNER ADVERTISING STRATEGY

### Targeting Recommendations

**Awareness Ads (Facebook Ad 1):**
- **Audience:** Parents 25-45, families, new residents in area
- **Interests:** Family health, parenting, wellness, local community
- **Placement:** Feed, Stories, Right Column

**Conversion Ads (Facebook Ad 2, Google Display):**
- **Audience:** People searching for "family doctor near me," "new patient appointments," "primary care physician"
- **Intent:** High intent, actively looking for healthcare provider
- **Placement:** Google Display Network (health-related content), Facebook Retargeting

**Telehealth Ads (Facebook Ad 3):**
- **Audience:** Busy professionals 28-50, parents with young children, remote workers
- **Interests:** Productivity, work-from-home, convenience, time management
- **Placement:** Feed, Stories, In-Stream Video

### A/B Testing Recommendations

Test variations of:
1. **CTA language:** "Book Now" vs "Get Started" vs "Schedule Appointment"
2. **Visual style:** Photo vs illustration vs gradient
3. **Headline emphasis:** Service-focused vs benefit-focused
4. **Color schemes:** Blue-dominant vs green-dominant
5. **Social proof:** With vs without ratings/testimonials

### Performance Metrics to Track

- **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Target 1.5-2.5% for awareness, 2.5-4% for conversion
- **Conversion Rate:** Target 10-15% for appointment bookings
- **Cost Per Click (CPC):** Benchmark against local healthcare advertising norms
- **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA):** Track cost per new patient appointment
- **Engagement Rate:** Especially for awareness campaigns
