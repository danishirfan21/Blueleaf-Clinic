### Pro Tips for Portfolio Presentation:

**Consistency:**
- Use consistent background colors across mockups (white, light gray, or brand color)
- Maintain similar lighting and shadow styles
- Create a cohesive visual story

**Quality:**
- All images minimum 3000px wide for Behance (2400px minimum for other platforms)
- High resolution, crisp, professional
- No pixelation or artifacts

**Context:**
- Include brief text descriptions for each mockup explaining the application
- Share design decisions: why this color, why this typeface, how it solves client needs

**Storytelling:**
- Begin with problem/brief: "Blueleaf Clinic needed a modern, approachable brand identity..."
- Show process: mood board, sketches, iterations (if desired)
- Present final work: mockups showcase the solution
- Close with results/impact: "The new brand positions Blueleaf as a modern, trusted healthcare partner..."

## MOCKUP COMPOSITION TIPS

### Lighting:
- Consistent lighting across all mockups (soft, natural, not harsh shadows)
- Avoid overly dark or overly bright
- Professional studio lighting look

### Backgrounds:
- **Solid colors:** White, light gray (#F5F5F5), Warm Cream (#F8F6F3)
- **Gradients:** Blueleaf Blue to Fresh Leaf Green (subtle, not overpowering)
- **Textured:** Light wood, marble, concrete (subtle, not distracting)
- **Contextual:** Real environments (clinic, office, outdoors) when appropriate

### Angles & Perspective:
- Slight angle (not flat-on) adds depth and interest
- Consistent perspective across mockups in the same scene
- Avoid extreme angles that distort the design

### Props (Use Sparingly):
- Plants (connects to "leaf" branding, adds life)
- Coffee cup (lifestyle, everyday context)
- Notebook, pen (work context)
- Keep props minimal and neutral (don't distract from main design)

### Shadows:
- Soft, realistic shadows add depth
- Avoid harsh, fake-looking drop shadows
- Consistent shadow direction (light source from same angle)

## EXAMPLE PORTFOLIO DESCRIPTION (FOR BEHANCE)

**Project Title:**
Blueleaf Clinic — Complete Branding, Identity System & Digital Marketing Asset Pack (2025)

**Project Description:**

Blueleaf Clinic is a modern family healthcare practice dedicated to providing compassionate, comprehensive care for every generation. They came to me needing a complete brand identity that would position them as both trustworthy and approachable—a balance often missing in the healthcare industry.

**The Challenge:**
Most healthcare brands lean too clinical (cold, sterile, intimidating) or too casual (unprofessional, not trustworthy). Blueleaf needed to strike the perfect balance: professional expertise with human warmth.

**The Solution:**
I created a complete brand identity system centered around the concept of growth and natural care. The name "Blueleaf" combines the trust and professionalism of blue with the nurturing, organic quality of a leaf—perfectly capturing their approach to healthcare.

**Deliverables:**
- Complete brand identity (logo, color system, typography, iconography)
- Social media content kit (Instagram/Facebook posts with full copy and design specs)
- Digital ad campaign (Facebook ads, Google Display ads, YouTube banner, website hero)
- Lead magnet ebook design ("10 Smart Ways to Improve Your Health Today")
- Brand guidelines document
- Environmental and print applications

**The Result:**
A warm, modern brand that feels both medically credible and genuinely caring—inviting families to find their healthcare home at Blueleaf Clinic.
